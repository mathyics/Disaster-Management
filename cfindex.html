Disaster Management


Hurricanes are powerful tropical storms with strong winds, heavy rain, and potential flooding, often causing widespread damage and coastal erosion.

Earthquakes are sudden ground shakes caused by the release of energy from tectonic plate movements, often causing destruction.

Wildfires are uncontrolled fires that spread rapidly through vegetation, fueled by dry conditions, wind, and heat, causing significant destruction.

Landslides are rapid movements of rock, soil, or debris down slopes, triggered by gravity, rain, earthquakes, or human activities

Floods are the overflow of water onto normally dry land, often caused by heavy rain, river overflow, or storm surges, leading to damage and displacement
